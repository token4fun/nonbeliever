Frequently Asked Questions

What is NonBeliever Coin?
NonBeliever Coin is a unique cryptocurrency project that embraces the idea of waiting—with a futuristic neon aesthetic. We combine cutting‐edge technology with a playful approach to market timing.
How do I solve the Enigma?
To solve the Enigma, click on the "Try to Solve" button on our homepage. Enter your answer along with your Telegram username and email. Your submission is processed and a confirmation is sent via our system.
Where can I find the latest updates?
Updates are available through our Crypto Ticker, our homepage, and our social media channels. We also publish a public Google Spreadsheet with user responses and FAQs for transparency.
How are the responses organized?
All submissions via the Enigma form are collected in our public Google Spreadsheet. We analyze the responses periodically to address common questions and improve our project.
Who can I contact for support?
For further assistance, please visit our Contact page or reach out to us via our official social media channels.
